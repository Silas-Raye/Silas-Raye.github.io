Game Design Portfolio

Game design is my creative escape—a hobby where I fuse logic and imagination. My projects, like Boom + Bust (a solo-coded puzzle game about demolishing bank vaults) and The Observer (a narrative experiment built with a volunteer team), let me explore mechanics and storytelling. While I've custom-drawn some art or collaborated with others, I often blend in AI-generated visuals—my true passion lies in coding and system design. Every line of code, concept, and gameplay loop are entirely my own.
